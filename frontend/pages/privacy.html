Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 7, 2026 · Last Updated: March 7, 2026 · Version: MVP / Beta Release

This Privacy Policy explains how LOADFITCO ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you use the LOAD. mobile application, website, and wearable device (collectively, the "Service").

By accessing or using the Service, you signify that you have read, understood, and agree to our collection, storage, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

A. Account Details & Personal Information

When you create an account or interact with our Service, we collect:

  • Name and Email address (e.g., via Google Sign-In)
  • Age, Height, Weight, and Gender (vital for algorithmic accuracy)
  • Profile picture (if provided)

B. Health, Fitness, and Sensor Data

Our core functionality relies on processing physical data collected by the LOAD. wearable or integrated services (like Google Fit or Apple Health):

  • Heart rate and Heart Rate Variability (HRV)
  • Blood Oxygen levels (SpO₂)
  • Step count and general activity tracking
  • Sleep stages and duration
  • Derived metrics (Recovery Score, Strain Score, Oxygen Stability)

C. Device and Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information when you access the Service:

  • Device model, OS version, and unique device identifiers
  • IP address, network details, and general location (not precise GPS)
  • App usage logs, interaction data, and diagnostic data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide the Service: To generate your recovery and performance insights, sync your wearable data, and maintain your account.
  • Improve Algorithms: To refine our health and fitness models, detect anomalies, and improve the accuracy of our insights (data used for algorithmic training is aggressively aggregated and de-identified).
  • Personalize Experience: To provide actionable coaching recommendations (e.g., "Rest Day" vs "Train Hard") tailored to your unique baseline.
  • Communication: To send account updates, technical notices, and security alerts.

We will strictly NEVER sell your personal health or biometric data to third parties.

3. How We Share Your Information

We only share your data in the following restricted circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use trusted third-party services (e.g., cloud hosting, email delivery) to operate our app. They are bound by strict data processing agreements.
  • Third-Party Integrations: If you explicitly opt-in to connect LOAD. with services like Google Fit or Apple Health, we will synchronize your data according to their respective policies.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law, court order, or overriding regulatory request.
  • Anonymized Data: We may share aggregated, completely de-identified data for research, marketing, or analytical purposes. It is impossible to link this data back to you.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is encrypted both in transit (using protocols like TLS/SSL) and at rest on secure cloud servers. We implement stringent, industry-standard access controls to prevent unauthorized access.

Despite our best efforts, no system is perfectly secure. By using the Service, you acknowledge that you provide your data at your own risk.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal information only for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you the Service.

You can request the complete deletion of your account and all associated health data at any time from within the app settings or by contacting us. Upon deletion request, your identifiable data is wiped from our active databases within 30 days.

6. Third-Party API Policies (Google Fit)

LOAD. connects with Google Fit API to give you a holistic view of your health. Our use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18 without parental consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have inadvertently collected such data, we will aggressively delete it.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you within the app or by email. Your continued use of the Service after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
